About Me

Hi, I’m Nikos, a graduate of Le Monde with over 20 years of experience in the culinary world. I’ve worked in Michelin-starred restaurants and prestigious hotels. My cooking blends Mediterranean flavors with French techniques and Japanese-Peruvian touches inspired by Matsuhisa by Nobu in Athens and Nobu London. I chose Personal Cooking to connect directly with my guests and express the artistry of cooking in a more intimate way. Enjoy a warm, relaxed, and unforgettable culinary experience at home – one you’ll be eager to repeat.
